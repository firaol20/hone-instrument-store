========================================================
HONE STORE - TELEGRAM BOT SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
========================================================

Follow these exact steps to connect your real store channel to the website and ensure the bot acts as your silent manager, sending you private reports about uploaded products.

Because of our recent updates, the bot will NEVER reply in the public channel. It will only send success/error logs directly to you (the admin).

--------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: Add the Bot to Your Real Channel
--------------------------------------------------------
1. Open Telegram and go to your real Hone store channel.
2. Tap on the channel name at the top to open the info screen.
3. Tap "Administrators" -> "Add Admin".
4. Search for your bot's username (the one you created in BotFather).
5. Add it as an administrator. It MUST have the right to "Manage Messages" (so it can read the posts).

--------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: Start a Private Chat with the Bot
--------------------------------------------------------
For the bot to send you private success/error logs, you must give it permission to message you directly:
1. Search for your bot in the main Telegram search bar.
2. Open the chat with your bot and tap "Start" (or type /start).
* Note: If you want a different admin to receive the logs, THEY must do this step instead.

--------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: Get Your Personal Telegram Chat ID
--------------------------------------------------------
The website needs to know exactly WHO the admin is so it can send the logs to the right place.
1. In Telegram, search for a bot called @userinfobot or @raw_data_bot.
2. Tap Start. It will instantly reply with your "Id" (a number like 6852989682).
3. Copy this number.

--------------------------------------------------------
STEP 4: Update Your Live Server (Render)
--------------------------------------------------------
1. Log in to your Render dashboard where your backend is hosted.
2. Go to your backend web service and click on "Environment Variables".
3. Look for the variable named: TELEGRAM_ADMIN_CHAT_ID
4. Paste the ID you copied in Step 3 as the value.
5. Click "Save Changes" (Render will automatically restart the backend).

--------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO TEST IT
--------------------------------------------------------
Once Render finishes restarting, go to your real channel and post a product using the exact format we established:

⭐️ KEYBOARDS
✅MODEL: Yamaha PSR-E373
✅CONDITION: New
✅PRICE: 25000

Great keyboard for beginners.


What will happen:
1. The post will appear in your public channel normally.
2. The bot will silently read it and upload it to your website.
3. The bot will then send a PRIVATE message directly to you saying: "✅ Successfully added product Yamaha PSR-E373". Your customers will never see this message.
